Getting Started

Sandbox is a personal miniapp platform that runs locally on your machine. To start the app, run python run.py from the project directory, then open http://localhost:8000 in your browser.

On Windows, you can copy start.bat.template to start.bat and double-click it for a quick launch shortcut.

Home Page

The home page displays all available miniapps as cards or list items. Use the sidebar to filter by tag, the search bar to find apps by name, and the sort dropdown to change the display order.

Miniapps

Each miniapp is a self-contained tool accessible from the home page. Click any app card to open it. Apps are organized by tags and can be favorited for quick access.

Tags

Tags categorize your miniapps. Click a tag in the sidebar to filter the home page to only show apps with that tag. You can create new tags and assign them to apps through the edit modal.

Tags can be reordered by dragging them in the sidebar.

Favorites

Click the star icon on any app card to mark it as a favorite. Favorited apps appear in a dedicated section at the top of the home page for quick access.

Search and Sort

Use the search bar in the top bar to filter apps by name or description. The sort dropdown lets you order apps by name, date added, or last used.

Card and List View

Toggle between card view (grid of cards with thumbnails) and list view (compact rows) using the view toggle buttons in the top bar. Your preference is saved locally.

Editing App Details

Hover over an app card and click the edit (pencil) icon to open the edit modal. From here you can change the app's title, description, assigned tags, and thumbnail image.

Thumbnails

Each app can have a custom thumbnail image displayed on its card. Upload a thumbnail through the edit modal. Images are stored in the static/thumbnails/ directory.
